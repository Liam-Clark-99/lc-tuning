Engine modifications

We offer a wide range of engine modifications including but not limited to:

  • Performance air intake systems
  • Fuel pump & line upgrades
  • Injector upgrades

All of our engine upgrades are tailor made to suit your car's individual profile to ensure the best results possible!

Remapping

We provide custom made engine and gearbox (subject to gearbox type) maps to ensure you're getting the best out of your cars hardware. It's not only performance that can be upgraded either - there is always the added benefit of an increased fuel consumption due to the improved use of the air/fuel mixture. A true win-win :)

Bodykiyts + Aero

If you want your car to stand out from the crowd then a custom made bodykit could be the perfect thing for you! All of our bodykits are made using the highest quality materials and you have the option of what material you wanted. Each kit is specifically designed to fit your car perfectly and is tailor made from start to finish.

Exhaust modifications

Want your ride to sound fruitier? Or maybe just to accentuate a particular exhaust note when under load? Our fully customisable exhaust systems could be just what you are looking for! You have the ability to pick and choose certain aspects of the exhaust all the way from the header pipes to the exhaust tips.
